What Ehsas Tool Do?

Ehsaas Tracking offers real-time monitoring of funds provided to deserving citizens through Income Support Programs. It connects with NADRA's CNIC database, allowing convenient monitoring and verification of Ehsaas applications. The Ehsaas Program aims to:

  • Create Safety Nets
  • Enhance Financial Inclusion
  • Promote Digital Orientation
  • Empower Women Economically
  • Eradicate Poverty
  • Drive Economic Growth
  • Foster Human Capital Formation
  • Support Sustainable Development
  • Ensure Access to Health Facilities
  • Provide Post-Secondary Education Support

Ehsaas Program includes various initiatives such as Ehsaas Tracking Web, Ehsaas Labour, Ehsaas Humqadam, and Ehsaas Masawaat, each with its eligibility criteria. Eligibility criteria may vary based on factors like income level, household size, CNIC, health, education, gender, geographic location, and community status.
